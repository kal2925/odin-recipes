Banana Bread Recipe

The beauty of this banana bread recipe is you don't need a fancy mixer to create a moist and delicous Banana Bread.!A mixing bowl, a fork to whisk the eggs and a strudy spoon to mix the batter with is all you need. Lets get started!

List Of Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 Medium very ripe bananas,peeled
  • 1/3 cup butter,unsalted or salted, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda (not baking powder)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar(1/2 cup if you would like it less sweet)
  • 1 large egg,beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C):

    2. Butter an 8x4-inch loaf pan.

  2. Mash the bananas and add the butter:

    3. In a mixing bowl, mash bananas with a fork until smooth. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas

  3. Mix in the remaining ingredients:

    4. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.

  4. Bake the bread:

    5. Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan. Baked for 55 to 65 minutes at specifed temperature or until toothpick insterted into the center comes out clean.

  5. Cool and Serve:

    6. Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then remove the banana bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving.
